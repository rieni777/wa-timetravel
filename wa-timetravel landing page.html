Jump to any date

Pick a day and see what was said that same day across the years you still have. Like time traveling. Not perfect, but it's something.

🔒

Nothing leaves your device

100% offline. Your exported chat file is read locally in your browser. No servers, no cloud, no one else touching it.

🔍

Search across everything

Find any word, any fragment of memory that survived. Navigate between results one by one.

📄

Save as PDF or HTML

Keep a version that doesn't depend on WhatsApp. For the chats you managed to rescue.

📱

Works on mobile + desktop

Built for Android WhatsApp exports right now. iOS support coming soon. Free and open source, forever.
